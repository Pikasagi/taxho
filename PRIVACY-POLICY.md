# Privacy Policy for TaxHo

Last updated: 2026-04-02

TaxHo ("the App") helps users record income and expenses for personal tax record-keeping. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

## 1. Data We Collect

### Data you provide in the App

- Expense and income records (amount, date, category, notes)
- Receipt or invoice files you attach
- App preferences (such as language and settings)

### Data processed for optional features

If you enable optional cloud AI fallback features, receipt-related content may be sent to the selected third-party AI provider to process extraction requests.

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Provide core app functionality (recording, summaries, export/import)
- Display and organize your records
- Power optional features you explicitly enable (such as cloud AI fallback)

## 3. Data Storage

- Your records are stored locally on your device by default.
- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not use your records for advertising profiling.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services only when required for enabled features, for example:

- Optional cloud AI provider integrations (if user enables fallback and configures credentials)

When you use third-party services, their privacy policies also apply.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

- When required to provide a feature you turn on (for example optional cloud AI processing)
- When required by law

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- You can edit or delete records at any time within the App.
- Deleting the App may remove locally stored data on your device (unless included in device backups).
- You are responsible for managing your own exported files and backups.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. International Users

If optional cloud services are enabled, data may be processed in regions where those providers operate.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published at this page with the updated date.
